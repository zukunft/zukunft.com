Concept
-------

One idea is to have an "all-in-one" concept for calculation and decisions. 
For calculation numbers are needed, but for humans numbers without words are a kind of useless, 
so all numbers are linked with a list of words. Words may have more than one meaning and 
to specify which meaning is relevant triples with subject, predicate and object are used. 

## Why not use pure RDF?

The [W3C RDF](https://www.w3.org/RDF/) seems to have the disadvantage that numbers are assigned with a property, which implies that 
the number of properties can be high which can lead to high storage usage (e.g. https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Wikidata:Property_proposal/number_of_comments_per_day)
